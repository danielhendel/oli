# Oli Bench Press Keyframe Candidate Review Checklist v1

**Status:** Authoritative for Bench Press imported keyframe human QA  
**Version:** bench-press-keyframe-qa-worksheet-v1

---

## Global Bench Press image QA criteria

- Full bench visible in 16:9
- Barbell and plates visible — no warped barbell
- Feet visible and planted
- No spotter in frame
- Premium dark Oli studio aesthetic
- No watermark, logos, or readable text
- No distorted hands or impossible anatomy
- Realistic equipment geometry
- Single still keyframe — no multiple motion phases

---

## setup

- Athlete lying on flat bench
- Eyes under or slightly behind bar
- Hands set evenly on bar
- Feet planted on floor
- Upper back tight — scapulae retracted
- Glutes on bench pad

---

## start_lockout

- Bar above chest/shoulder line
- Elbows locked or nearly locked
- Wrists stacked over elbows
- Scapulae retracted and depressed
- Stable torso on bench

---

## bottom_chest_pause

- Bar touches lower chest/sternum line
- Clear paused bottom position
- Wrists stacked at bottom
- Moderate elbow angle
- No bounce implied
- Bar not hovering above chest

---

## finish_lockout

- Bar returned to lockout
- No second descent implied
- Stable bar at finish
- Controlled confident finish
- Character, camera, and equipment continuity

---

## Hard gates

Critical items block master approval:
- No watermark
- No logos / readable text
- Correct exercise and character
- Realistic anatomy and equipment
- Rights review required
- Bench Press pose accuracy (per pose checklist)

---

## Rights / watermark checks

Imported candidates default to `internal-dev-only`.

Watermark or logos/readable text flags block `eligible-for-master-review`.

---

## Master approval out of scope for M15

Completing the worksheet does not approve the candidate.

`eligible-for-master-review` is a planning label only — not `approved-master` status.

Image pack approval requires approved-master candidates in a future sprint.
